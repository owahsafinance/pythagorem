Overview

YAVAXLEND (XLEND) is the first truly crypto-collateralized lending and borrowing platform. While other platforms peg the price of collateralized capital to their USD values, YAVAXLEND will be pegged directly to tokens themselves. Users will be able to mint synthetic assets (synTokens) equivalent to a percentage of the base token. For example, if a user contributes 10 XLEND tokens, they will receive anywhere from 10-90% of the contributed capital, depending on two factors.

  1. The price volatility of the underlying asset
  2. Global rules passed down by XLEND Governance
